Movie Rankings

  1. Revenge of the Sith
    • Several of the best lightsaber duels in Star Wars cinematic history
    • Shows how intricate and in-depth the Emperor's plot was to overthrow the Jedi and rule the galaxy
    • Best overall film score for all Star Wars movies due to emotional connection with the scenes portrayed
  2. Rogue One
    • Very fast-paced and action packed, showing how quickly the Rebels needed to move in order to find hope in destroying the Death Star and overthrowing the Empire
    • Great action and several plot twists due to intricate characters and their need for survival
    • Likeable characters that you just can't help getting connected to, no matter how short-lived
  3. Empire Strikes Back
    • Shows the brutality and ruthlessness of the Empire and its full capabilities
    • Shows part of the training that Jedi go through in order to hone in their skills and truly become masters
    • Compelling character development for all main characters involved
